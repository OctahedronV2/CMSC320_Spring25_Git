Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Because it allows us to easily share our code, but also use version control to be able to undo critical mistakes or revert changes.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: It could be applied to a lot of different fields, but the first one that comes to mind is Economics because we can use data science to analyze many different aspects of our economy, such as poverty rates, unemployment, etc.
